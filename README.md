# 📊 NaijaCart Business Intelligence Analysis using SQL

## Overview

This project presents an end-to-end SQL analysis of **NaijaCart**, a Nigerian e-commerce company specializing in smartphones, consumer electronics, fashion, groceries, and household products.

The analysis evaluates the company's 2025 business performance against its strategic objectives by examining sales trends, customer behaviour, product performance, referral effectiveness, payment preferences, and operational efficiency. The insights generated are intended to support management's strategic planning for 2026.

---

## Business Problem

Although NaijaCart processed over **4,000 customer orders** during 2025, management lacked clear visibility into the factors driving revenue growth, customer acquisition, referral performance, regional sales, and operational efficiency.

This project answers these business questions using SQL and provides data-driven recommendations to support future business decisions.

---

## Business Objectives

This analysis aims to:

- Evaluate overall sales performance.
- Identify revenue-driving products and categories.
- Analyse customer purchasing behaviour.
- Assess the effectiveness of the referral programme.
- Evaluate payment method preferences.
- Assess delivery and operational performance.
- Provide actionable business recommendations.

## Key Business Insights

- Generated **₦2.81 Billion** in total revenue during 2025.
- **Electronics** was the highest revenue-generating category.
- **Niger State** generated the highest revenue among all states.
- **December** recorded the highest sales volume.
- Referral customers generated approximately **₦995 Million** in revenue.
- **OPay** was the most frequently used payment method.
- **719 orders** remained undelivered, indicating operational improvement opportunities.
